ITALIAN DISHES RECIPE

Pasta carbonara

Pasta alla Carbonara is a classic Italian dish from Rome, made with spaghetti, eggs, guanciale, Pecorino Romano, and black pepper. It's creamy without using cream, showcasing the simplicity and rich flavors of Italian cooking.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 egg yolks + 1 whole egg
  • 50g guanciale (or pancetta)
  • 40g Pecorino Romano, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt (for pasta water)

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Save ½ cup of pasta water.
  2. While pasta cooks, fry guanciale in a pan until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs, cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Drain pasta and mix quickly with guanciale (remove from heat).
  5. Stir in egg mixture, adding pasta water a little at a time until creamy.
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.

Serving size:

2 pepple

Pizza

Italian Pizza is a world-famous dish known for its thin, crispy crust, simple toppings, and rich tomato flavor. Originating from Naples, traditional Italian pizza celebrates quality ingredients and balance in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 325ml warm water
  • 10g salt
  • 10g salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 200g canned San Marzano tomatoes
  • 200g fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, yeast, salt, water, and olive oil to form a dough. Knead for 10 minutes.
  2. Let the dough rise for 1–2 hours until doubled in size.
  3. Preheat oven to highest setting (250–280°C) with a pizza stone or tray inside.
  4. Divide dough into two, stretch into thin rounds.
  5. Spread crushed tomatoes, add mozzarella and basil.
  6. Bake for 7–10 minutes until crust is golden and cheese melted.

Serving size:

2 pizzas

Ravioli

Ravioli is a traditional Italian pasta filled with ingredients like cheese, meat, or vegetables, often served with a simple sauce. It’s a comforting and versatile dish that highlights the heart of Italian home cooking.

Ingredients

  • 200g flour (preferably 00)
  • 2 eggs
  • 150g ricotta cheese
  • 50g spinach (cooked and chopped)
  • 30g grated Parmesan
  • Salt, pepper, and nutmeg to taste
  • Butter and sage (for sauce)

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and eggs to form a dough, knead until smooth, rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Mix ricotta, spinach, Parmesan, salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg for filling.
  3. Roll out dough thinly, place small filling mounds on one sheet, cover with another, press and cut into ravioli.
  4. Boil ravioli for 3–4 minutes in salted water.
  5. Melt butter in a pan with fresh sage, then toss in the ravioli.
  6. Serve hot with extra Parmesan.

Serving size:

2 servings

DESSERT RECIPE

Panna cotta

Panna cotta is a classic Italian dessert made from sweetened cream thickened with gelatin. It’s silky, smooth, and often served with fruit, caramel, or chocolate sauce.

Ingredients

  • 250ml heavy cream
  • 40g sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 sheet (or 2g) gelatin
  • Fresh berries or sauce for topping

Instructions

  1. Soak gelatin in cold water for 5–10 minutes.
  2. Heat cream and sugar in a saucepan until just boiling, then remove from heat.
  3. Add vanilla and the softened gelatin; stir until dissolved.
  4. Drain pasta and mix quickly with guanciale (remove from heat).
  5. Pour into molds or cups and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  6. Serve chilled with toppings like berries or berry coulis.

Serving size:

2 servings

Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a beloved Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cream, and cocoa powder. It’s rich, creamy, and perfectly balanced between sweet and bitter flavors.

Ingredients

  • 150g mascarpone cheese
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 100ml strong coffee (cooled)
  • 6 ladyfinger biscuits
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions

  1. Whisk egg yolk with sugar until pale, then fold in mascarpone.
  2. Dip ladyfingers briefly in coffee and place in a serving dish.
  3. Add a layer of mascarpone cream, then repeat with another layer.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
  5. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.

Serving size:

2 servings
